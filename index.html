An Analysis of the Impact Engine Cylinders has on MPG

This website is dedicated to exploring the impact that engine cylinders have on a car's miles per gallon (mpg). The two types of mpg that will be analyzed here are AverageHighwayMPG (gas mileage of a car when they continuously drive at a high speed) and AverageCityMPG (gas mileage of a car when they are in start and stop traffic). We invite the user to explore this web page further to discover the impact that a higher number of engine cylinders has on these two statistics, as well as how a higher number of engine cylinders affect the difference between the two values.
